services

Start Reaching out effectively on all devices

I enjoy keeping up to date with the latest web design trends. In the past five years the way people view and interact with websites has changed which has led to the growth of responsive design. I care about design and I like to ensure that all websites I design can be viewed on all devices, from desktop browser to mobile and that the user experience is not affected by the nature of the device used.

Web Design

A website can look fantastic but if the site is not goal orientated then all that time spent making it look amazing will be in vain. Therefore it’s important to understand what the ambitions of the website are right from the start. Before even opening up Photoshop it’s vital to start with a two-way process of consultation and wireframing, taking on the client’s feedback and revising the design in order to deliver the best outcome for your website.

Content Management Systems

Publish content when you want and where you want, that is the difference between having a static website and a dynamic website that is run on a Content Management System (CMS). Having a CMS in place is an important part of having a content strategy that can help you target the right audience and help drive your SEO.

Hosting & Domains

Does your new website needs a home? I can offer Domain name and hosting service that is reliable, affordable and secure.

Branding & Corporate Identity

As one of the most important components to any business it’s essential that your brand connects to your target audience and expresses your company's values to the consumer.

Training

About

Design adds value faster than it adds costs Thomas C. Gale

Are you looking for creative, unique designs to help define your online brand and make you stand out from your competitors?

Read my CV Get in touch

I have nine years' experience in bringing together digital technologies and design to create something fresh and distinctive. Having worked in the world of web design, graphic design and video production for many years, my work has been featured in Web Design Magazine.

Previous work experience includes developing software, building websites, filming and editing digital video for the University of Edinburgh.

More recently, I worked with a top digital agency taking design specifications and making them an online reality. This provided me with in-depth experience of producing high-quality websites for blue-chip clients.

Currently I am available for commissioned work, including WordPress websites, innovative digital design, and comprehensive branding strategies. Feel free to check out my portfolio of completed work to see if we'd make a good match.

Start Reaching out effectively on all devices

I care about design and I like to ensure that all websites I design can be viewed on all devices, from desktop browser to mobile and that the user experience is not affected by the nature of the device used.
